Project Title: Analysis of human evaluation of different types of information in different environments

Principal Investigator: Ilya Monosov

Research Team Contact: Beiya Xu - (443) 418-2841 - bxu41@jh.edu

We invite you to participate in this research study because you are a healthy adult between the ages of 18-55. The purpose of this research study is to investigate how people factor uncertainty and information into their choices.

If you agree to participate, we would like you to complete one or more computerized tasks that will involve viewing stimuli on the screen (e.g., shapes, words, images, complex patterns) and indicating a response based on the task instructions. Responses may involve making choices, engaging in a mental process and/or carrying out a physical action. Some of these tasks may offer additional incentives above your base pay (see Compensation). You may also be asked to complete brief questionnaires assessing your subjective experiences, various general preferences, and demographic information. You are free to skip any questions that you prefer not to answer in the questionnaire. The entire procedure should last 120-180 minutes.

We will not ask you for additional consent for these uses of your information.

There are no known risks from being in this study, and you will not benefit personally. However we hope that others may benefit in the future from what we learn as a result of this study.

You will not have any costs for being in this research study.

You will be paid for being in this research study. Your Prolific ID will be recorded as a way for us to confirm you have completed the experiment and then properly compensate you. If you complete this experiment fully, will be paid a base amount of $XXX and may also receive bonus payment based on your performance, between $0 to $XXX.

We will keep the information you provide confidential by ensuring data are stored and transferred securely and by limiting access to the research team only. Your Prolific ID is deidentified, does not contain any information about your name, and will be dissociated from your responses and task data, though it is associated with your Prolific worker profile. Any report or article that we write will not include information that can directly identify you. The journals that publish these reports or articles require that we share your information that was collected for this study with others to make sure the results of this study are correct and help develop new ideas for research. Your information will be shared in a way that cannot directly identify you.

If you do not wish to participate in this study or want to end your participation in the study, simply close his window. Again, you will not be penalized or lose any benefits for which you otherwise qualify.

Also, at the end of the experiment we will ask you whether you are willing to participate in our other studies in the future. If you choose not to participate, we won't contact you after this study. If you choose participate, we will send you a request for doing a similar task.

We encourage you to ask questions. If you have any questions about the research study itself, please feel free to contact the research team by phone at (443) 418-2841 or by email at bxu41@jh.edu. If you feel that you have been harmed in any way by your participation in this study, please contact Ilya Monosov at (314) 362-3740.
